Activity 3.1.2.

Suppose we wanted to solve the linear program:
\begin{align*} \textbf{Maximize: } f(\mathbf{x})= 5x+4y \amp \\ \textbf{Subject to: } 2x+3y\amp \leq 26\\ -2x -10 y\amp \leq 2 \end{align*}

(a)

We can record the program as follows:
\(x \) \(y \) \(-1\)
\(2\) \(3 \) \(26\) \(=-t_1\)
\(-2\) \(-10 \) \(2 \) \(=-t_2\)
\(5 \) \(4 \) \(0\) \(=f\)
Out of \(x_y, y, t_1, t_2\text{,}\) which are non-negative?

(b)

Perform a pivot on the given entry.
\(\ec{x} \) \(\ec{y} \) \(-1\)
\(2^*\) \(3 \) \(26\) \(=-t_1\)
\(-2\) \(-10 \) \(2 \) \(=-t_2\)
\(5 \) \(4 \) \(0\) \(=f\)

(c)

Consider the resulting tableau:
\(t_2 \) \(\ec{y} \) \(-1\)
\(?\) \(? \) \(?\) \(=-\ec{x}\)
\(?\) \(? \) \(? \) \(=-t_2\)
\(? \) \(? \) \(?\) \(=f\)
What point are we on?

(d)

Why is this point NOT optimal?

(e)

  1. \(t_1=0\text{.}\)
  2. \(t_1=10\text{.}\)
