Theorem 4.2.4. The Farkas Lemma.

Given a matrix \(A\in \R^{n\times m}\) and \(\mathbf{b}\in \R^n\text{,}\) exactly one of the following is true:
  1. There is a \(\mathbf{x}\in \R^m\) such that \(\mathbf{x}\geq \mathbf{0}\) and \(A\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{b}\)
  2. There is a \(\mathbf{y}\in \R^n\) such that \(\y^\top \vb\lt 0\) and \(\y^\top A \geq \mathbf{0}\text{.}\)
