Let \(N\) be a capacitated network with a unique source and sink, with no edges going into the source or out of the sink. Let \(x_{ij}\) be a flow on this network with value \(f\) then prove that
\begin{equation*} f=\sum_{i\in V_1, j\in V_2}x_{ij}-\sum_{i\in V_1, j\in V_2}x_{ji}. \end{equation*}
